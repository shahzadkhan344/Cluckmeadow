About Us

Cluck Meadow Farms (OPC) Private Limited

Operates in the non- vegetarian food processing sector, specializing in raising chickens for meat production. The company not only focuses on the healthy growth of its chickens through natural feeding practices but also processes their waste into organic fertilizer. This dual approach not only ensures high-quality poultry products for consumers but also provides farmers with an environmentally friendly and cost-effective fertilizer solution, enhancing agricultural productivity and promoting sustainable farming practices.
